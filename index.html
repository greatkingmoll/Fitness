Eccentric Program

Negative Pull-ups 5s descent, control lowering. (3x 3-5, Jump or step up to start)
Nordic Hamstring Curls 4-5s negative. (3 x3-5, band assisted)
Step-Down Negatives 3s descent from 18" box. (3x 8-10/leg)
Eccentric Push-ups 4s lower, feet elevated. (3x 5-8, 4-second lowering, elevate feet if needed)
Single-Leg RDL Negative 3s hinge descent. (3x 6-8/leg)

Eccentric Programming Notes:

  • Progress by increasing time uinder tension, not just load
  • Focus on control rather than maximum resistance

Heavy Lifting

Deadlift Neutral spine, bar close, drive hips. (4x 3-5 reps, 80-85% 1RM, 3-4 min rest)
Goblet Squat Chest up, depth to parallel. (3x 5-8 reps, 70-80% 1RM, 2-3 min rest)
Farmer's Carry Brace core, steady pace. (3x 5-8, Body Weight + assistance, 2-3 min rest)
Assisted Pull-ups Full range, scapula retracted. (3x 8-12, progressive difficulty, 90 sec rest)
Push-up Variant Full depth, core tight. (3x 30-60 sec, 75-100% Body Weight, 2 min rest)

Progressive Loading

  • Deadlift Goal: Work up to bodyweight for 10 clean reps
  • Farmer's Carry Goal: Carry total bodyweight (split between hands) for 1+ minute
  • Pull-up Goal: Dead hang for 2 minutes (at age 40, reducing slightly per decade)

Key Form Priorities

  • Eccentric Control: 2-3 second lowering phase on all lifts
  • Full Range of Motion: Emphasize complete muscle lengthening
  • Intra-abdominal Pressure: Proper bracing and breathing technique
  • Progressive Overload: Increase load by 2.5-5lbs weekly, or add 1 rep when form is perfect
